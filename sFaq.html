Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the healthcare system

How do I schedule an appointment?
You can easily schedule an appointment by navigating to the Dashboard and clicking on the "Request Appointment" button. Fill in the required details and confirm.
What services does Healthcare Pro offer?
We offer general physician consultations, mental health counseling, emergency response, and digital prescription tracking for all university students.
Are consultations confidential?
Yes, absolutely. All your medical records, visit history, and consultation details are kept strictly confidential and secure within our system.
What are the consultation fees?
Standard consultations for enrolled university students are completely free of charge. Specialized treatments or external tests may incur separate costs.
How can I access my medical records?
You can access all your past prescriptions and medical visit history by navigating to the "Prescriptions" or "My Profile" tab from the sidebar.
What should I do in case of emergency?
Please visit the "Emergency" tab immediately. You will find national hotlines, ambulance contacts, and the nearest hospitals along with their direct contact numbers.
