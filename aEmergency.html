Emergency Response Protocol

Follow these steps to handle medical emergencies effectively

Step 1

Stay Calm and Assess

Take a deep breath. Quickly assess the situation and ensure your own safety first.

Step 2

Call Emergency Services

Dial 999 for immediate help. Provide clear location and nature of emergency.

Step 3

Provide First Aid

If trained, provide basic first aid. Do not move the patient unless necessary.

Step 4

Contact Nearest Hospital

Call the nearest hospital from the list below to alert them of incoming emergency.

Step 5

Document Everything

Note down time, symptoms, actions taken. This info is crucial for medical staff.

Step 6

Follow Up

After emergency is handled, ensure proper documentation and follow-up care.
