Common Questions

Browse through our most frequently asked questions below

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through the patient portal or by contacting our reception desk at +1 234 567 8900. Appointments are available Monday-Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

What services does Healthcare Pro offer?

Healthcare Pro provides comprehensive medical services including general consultations, specialized treatments, emergency support, and digital health record management.

Are consultations confidential?

Yes, all consultations and medical records are strictly confidential and follow industry-standard security protocols to protect your privacy.

What are the consultation fees?

Consultation fees vary depending on the department and specialist. You can view the fee structure in the Payment Management section of your portal.

How can I access my medical records?

Medical records can be accessed 24/7 through the "Patients" section in the Admin Portal or via the Student/Patient Portal using your login credentials.

What should I do in case of emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, please call our 24/7 emergency hotline at 999 or visit the "Emergency" section in the portal for nearest hospital details.

Still have questions?
